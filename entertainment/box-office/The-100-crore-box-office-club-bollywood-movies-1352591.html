The 100 Crore Net India Box Office Club - Indian Movies (Hindi Version): From Ghajini to War 2

So far, 122 Indian movies have managed to be part of the Rs 100 crore net India club for their Hindi Version. The club was inaugurated by Ghajini and War 2 is the newest addition.

The Rs 100 crore India net club in Hindi, for an Indian film, was inaugurated by Ghajini in 2008. It is Ghajini that made box office comparisons and discussions mainstream. Since then, 122movies have made it to the coveted list, the latest being War 2. While the Rs 100 crore club may have lost its relevance, it is still a prestige for many indiefilmmakers.
