The 200 Crore Net India Box Office Club - Indian Movies (Hindi Version): From 3 Idiots to Saiyaara

So far, 39 Indian movies have managed to be part of the Rs 200 crore net India club for their Hindi Version. The club was inaugurated by 3 Idiots and Saiyaara is the newest addition.

The Rs 200 crore net India club in Hindi among Indian movies was inaugurated by Rajkumar Hirani's 3 Idiots, starring Aamir Khan, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Sharman Joshi, R Madhavan and others. It took almost 4 years for another Indian movie to net Rs 200 crore in India in Hindi. Shah Rukh Khan led Chennai Express surpassed 3 Idiots, which was then surpassed by Dhoom 3.

Advertisement

Over the years, we have had 39Indian movies that havemanaged to be part of the Rs 200 crore net India club for their Hindi Version. The latest movie to be part of the Rs 200 crore club is Saiyaara. Saiyaara is among the highest grossing Indian movies for the Hindi language in India.
